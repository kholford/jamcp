Month 3 → Year ~6.5: Private Pay Phase

Monthly Burn

  • $7,700

Spend Down Progression — Remaining Assets by Year

  • Year 1:
    $610,200 - ($7,700 × 12 = $92,400) = $517,800

  • Year 2:
    $517,800 - $92,400 = $425,400

  • Year 3:
    $425,400 - $92,400 = $333,000

  • Year 4:
    $333,000 - $92,400 = $240,600

  • Year 5:
    $240,600 - $92,400 = $148,200

  • Year 6:
    $148,200 - $92,400 = $55,800

  • Year 6.5:
    $55,800 - ($7,700 × 6 = $46,200) = ~$9,600 (~depletion)

Year ~6.5: Medicaid (Title 19) Begins

  • Assets effectively depleted
  • Social Security continues toward care
  • Medicaid covers remaining cost

Year ~6.5 → End of Life: Medicaid Phase

  • Ongoing care funded by Medicaid
  • No remaining estate for inheritance
